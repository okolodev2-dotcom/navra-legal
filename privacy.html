Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Navra ("we", "our", "us") is a personal medication management app that helps patients track their medications and connect with family caregivers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Navra, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, age, sex, and role (Patient or Care Partner) provided during registration.
  • Medication Data: Medication names, dosages, prescription numbers, reminder schedules, and dose logs that you enter or scan.
  • Device Information: Expo push notification tokens for delivering medication reminders and alerts.
  • Photos: Images of medication labels captured through the in-app scanner. These are processed for text extraction and are not stored permanently on our servers.
  • Usage Data: Dose adherence records, pairing history, and activity logs within the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide medication tracking, reminders, and dose logging.
  • To enable pairing between patients and their chosen Care Partners.
  • To send push notifications for medication reminders, missed dose alerts, and Care Partner activity.
  • To check for potential drug interactions using the FDA OpenFDA API.
  • To generate plain-language medication descriptions using AI.
  • To calculate and display dose adherence statistics.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Your data is shared only in these limited circumstances:

  • With your Care Partner: If you voluntarily pair with a Care Partner, they can see your medication list, dose adherence, and receive notifications about your medications. You can unpair at any time.
  • Third-party services: We use Supabase for secure data storage and authentication, the FDA OpenFDA API for drug information and interaction checking, Anthropic's Claude API for generating medication descriptions, and Expo for push notification delivery.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Database access is controlled through Row Level Security (RLS) policies ensuring users can only access their own data. API keys are secured server-side through Supabase Edge Functions.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account through the app's Settings, all your personal data, medication records, dose logs, and pairing history will be permanently deleted.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you (visible in the app).
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time through Settings.
  • Unpair from a Care Partner at any time, revoking their access to your medication data.
  • Control push notification preferences through the app's Settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Navra is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Not a Medical Device

Navra is a personal medication management tool. It is not a medical device, does not provide medical advice, and should not be used as a substitute for professional medical guidance. Drug interaction information is provided for informational purposes only.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last updated" date above.

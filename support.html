Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pair with a Care Partner?
Go to the QR tab in your Navra app. If you're a patient, scan your Care Partner's QR code. If you're a Care Partner, show your QR code to the patient so they can scan it.

My notifications aren't working.
Open the Settings app on your phone, find Navra, and make sure Notifications are turned on. Also check that Dose Reminders are enabled inside Navra's own Settings screen.

How do I scan a pill bottle?
On the home screen, tap the camera icon next to "Add medication." Take a photo of the prescription label. Navra will extract the medication name, dosage, and directions automatically.

Is my data private?
Yes. Your medication list is only visible to you and any Care Partner you've explicitly paired with. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I remove a Care Partner?
Go to your Care Partner section on the home screen, long-press on their profile, and confirm the removal.
